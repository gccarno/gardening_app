Plants
  • Drag from Plant Library → bed grid to place in a bed cell
  • Drag from Plant Library → open canvas to place as a free circle
  • Click a placed plant → open care panel (watering, dates, notes)
  • Ctrl+click multiple plants → bulk update panel appears
  • Select All checkbox (beside "Plants in this Garden") → select all
  • Click × on a chip or circle → remove that plant
Beds
  • Drag from Beds palette → canvas to place a bed
  • Drag a placed bed by its header to reposition (snaps to 1 ft grid)
  • Click × on a bed header → remove the bed from canvas
